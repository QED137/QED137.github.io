Probability theory is a branch of mathematics concerned with analyzing random events. The probability of an event \( A \) occurring is defined as:

\[ P(A) = \frac{\text{Number of Favorable Outcomes}}{\text{Total Number of Outcomes}} \]

Probability plays a fundamental role in **Statistics, AI, and Machine Learning**.

üìç Example

If we roll a fair six-sided die, the probability of rolling a **3** is:

\[ P(3) = \frac{1}{6} \]
