About Me

Welcome to my world of knowledge! I am Janmajay Kumar, a Physicist, Data Scientist, and AI Enthusiast dedicated to uncovering **actionable insights** from data.

🔭 Bridging Physics, Data Science, and AI

I hold a **Master’s degree in Physics from IIT Delhi** and a **Master’s in Astro and Particle Physics from the University of Tübingen**. My journey spans across **theoretical physics, computational modeling, and advanced data-driven decision-making**.

With a deep foundation in **Astrophysics, Particle Physics, and Statistical Inference**, I have transitioned into the world of **Data Science and AI**, where I apply **mathematical models, machine learning, and big data analytics** to solve real-world problems.

🚀 My Expertise

  • Physics & Computational Modeling: Monte Carlo simulations, numerical methods, high-energy physics
  • Machine Learning & AI: Neural networks, NLP, OpenAI APIs, generative AI, LLM-based applications
  • Data Science & Analytics: Extracting meaningful patterns, statistical modeling, ETL pipelines
  • Programming & Tools: Python, TensorFlow, SQL, Pandas, Docker, Neo4j, Google Cloud
